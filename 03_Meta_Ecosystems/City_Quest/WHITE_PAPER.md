# City Quest — White Paper

> *A 14-app global citizenship platform where solidarity is structurally embedded in every transaction.*

---

## 1. The Problem

Modern civic life is fragmented. People participate in work, community, art, governance, and ecology through systems that are siloed, extractive, and designed to capture attention rather than build commons. Cities have no operating system. Citizens have no coordination layer. Solidarity remains an intention, never a mechanism.

---

## 2. The Vision

City Quest is the **operating system for a city built on solidarity** — a 14-app ecosystem that gives citizens tools to participate in, contribute to, and benefit from local and global civic life, with Fair BEAT redistribution embedded in every economic interaction.

---

## 5. Core Principle

For every City Quest feature, ask: **"Where does the solidarity happen in this code?"**

If the answer requires human goodwill — redesign until it doesn't.

---

## 6. Solidarity Mechanics

Fair BEAT runs at the smart contract level. Redistribution happens before any human can withhold it:

```
Any payment in City Quest
  → Smart contract intercepts
  → 93% → recipient
  → 7%  → Community Pool (automatic, enforced)
```
