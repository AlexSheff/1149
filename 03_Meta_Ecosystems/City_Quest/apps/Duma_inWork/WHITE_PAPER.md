# Duma inWork — White Paper

> *The bridge between City Quest's digital governance layer and Dumaguete's real civic infrastructure.*

---

## What It Does

### Governance Transparency
- Integrates public barangay council agendas and minutes
- Makes them searchable, subscribable, commentable by citizens
- Tracks which issues were raised by citizens vs. initiated by officials

### Participation Bridge
- Citizens file formal community input on open agenda items
- Input is Trustnet-signed (verifiable as from a real, accountable community member)
- Input aggregated and scored by community weight (Choose&Own stake)

### Principle
Not a replacement for government — a transparency and participation layer alongside it.

### Dumaguete First
Chosen as pilot for specific reasons:
- Manageable scale (~130,000 people)
- Active UU community network with existing civic relationships
- Good Project Corp has local organizational capacity

---

## Solidarity Mechanism

Accountability through visibility. Formal participation pathways, not just noise.
