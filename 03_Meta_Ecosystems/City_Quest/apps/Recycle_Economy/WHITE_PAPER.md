# Recycle Economy — White Paper

> *A circular economy protocol that makes ecological behavior economically rational.*

---

## Flow
```
Household sorts waste → QR-tagged bag at collection point
  → Collector scans QR → confirms receipt
  → Tokens earned (per kg, per category)
  → Processor confirms quality
  → Monthly community target hit → bonus pool released
  → Fair BEAT distributes bonus by Trustnet score
```

### Token Economics
- Value fixed relative to community treasury
- Redeemable at JobAround, Choose&Own, or partner businesses
- Non-transferable to external markets (prevents financialization of ecology)

### Dumaguete Pilot
3 local partner businesses confirmed. Phase 1: 50 households, 3 collection points, 1 processor.

---

## Solidarity Mechanism

Community bonus pool triggered by collective achievement — everyone is motivated by everyone else's participation.
