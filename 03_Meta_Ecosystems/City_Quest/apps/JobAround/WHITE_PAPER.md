# JobAround — White Paper

> *A hyperlocal work marketplace where fair pay is enforced by the protocol, not by goodwill.*

---

## The Vision

- Platform fee: **0%** (replaced by Fair BEAT 7% community contribution)
- Ratings: Trustnet-based — not gameable
- Pricing floors: set by community governance via Choose&Own
- Work history: portable, on-chain, belongs to the worker

### Payment Flow
```
Employer posts job → Workers apply (Trustnet score visible)
  → Employer selects → Escrow created on-chain
  → Work completed → Payment released
  → Fair BEAT: 93% worker / 7% community
  → Both parties' Trustnet scores updated
```

---

## Solidarity Mechanism

Zero extraction: the 7% Fair BEAT goes to the community, not to a platform. Workers keep 93%.
