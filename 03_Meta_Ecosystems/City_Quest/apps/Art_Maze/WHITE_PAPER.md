# Art Maze — White Paper

> *A creative economy platform where artists collaborate, earn, and own their work — without intermediaries.*

---

## Publication Flow
```
Artist creates work → Attribution declared (contributors + AI tools)
  → Published on Art Maze (IPFS storage)
  → Smart contract minted with royalty splits
  → Buyer purchases → Fair BEAT: 85% artist / 8% community / 7% protocol
  → Secondary sales: 10% royalty to original creator forever
```

### AI Art Attribution Standard
```json
{
  "human_creators": ["address"],
  "ai_tools": ["Recraft.ai", "Stable Diffusion"],
  "human_direction": "stored on IPFS",
  "copyright_holder": "address"
}
```

---

## Solidarity Mechanism

Secondary sale royalties return value to original creators forever. Community fund supports emerging artists.
