# Good Project Corp — White Paper

> *Philanthropic organizing structure with structural solidarity mechanics — not rhetoric, but architecture.*

## Mission

To build organizations where solidarity is not a value statement but an operational specification. Every legal structure, financial flow, and governance rule in Good Project Corp must mechanically enforce redistribution.

## Structure

- Legal entity in Philippines
- Governed by trust-weighted voting (Trustnet integration planned)
- Treasury managed by Fair BEAT rules
- Parallel coordination with 4Boon as impact investment arm

## Key Principle

If the organization's solidarity depends on the virtue of its current members, the organization will fail when those members leave. The structure must enforce solidarity without relying on virtue.
