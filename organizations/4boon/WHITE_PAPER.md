# 4Boon — White Paper

> *The impact investment arm of the Good Project ecosystem — capital directed by community values, not ROI alone.*

## 1. Vision

4Boon operates as a **structural complement to Good Project Corp**:
- Good Project Corp = civic organizing body (people, processes, governance)
- 4Boon = impact investment arm (capital, resources, financial flows)

Where Good Project Corp builds the community infrastructure, 4Boon directs resources into it — and tracks impact with the same rigor that financial investors track returns.

## 2. Model

**Not charity.** Not traditional investment. A hybrid:
- Invests in City Quest app deployments as impact projects
- Returns are measured in: community health metrics, participation rates, trust score growth
- Financial returns (if any) flow through Fair BEAT back to the community

## 3. Structure

- Operates under Philippine SEC registration (in progress)
- Governed by Diana Chua as impact strategy lead
- Coordinates with Good Project Corp via monthly alignment sessions
- Investment committee: trust-weighted, Trustnet-verified members

## 4. Current Focus

- City Quest Dumaguete pilot co-funding
- Recycle Economy infrastructure (collection points, processing contracts)
- UUCP Film Festival co-sponsorship
