# Philippines Relocation Log

> *1.8+ years in Dumaguete — documented relocation, deliberate rootedness, and local integration.*

---

## The Decision

The choice of Dumaguete was not accidental. It was a **deliberate act of rooting** — a conscious decision that global vision requires a specific physical anchor.

Dumaguete offered:
- Manageable scale (~130,000 people) for real civic experiments
- Active civil society: UU community, university presence, local business networks
- Accessible cost of living enabling extended deep work without financial pressure
- Geographic specificity that forces concrete implementation
- The particular quality of a city that has not yet been optimized for tourists

---

## What "Deliberate Rootedness" Means

This is not passing through. Dumaguete Quest pilot is not a project being done *in* Dumaguete — it is a project being done *with* Dumaguete.

This distinction matters:
- Passing through: extract what is useful, move on
- Deliberate rootedness: contribute in ways that outlast your presence
