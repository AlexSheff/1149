# AI Cinema Lab — White Paper

> *A dedicated production space for AI-augmented independent cinema — combining human creative vision with generative film technology.*

## 1. The Vision

AI Cinema Lab is the convergence point of three existing threads:
1. **UUCP Film Festival** — the community, the audience, the curatorial identity
2. **AI Music Pipeline** — the soundtrack and audio production tools
3. **Art Maze** — the distribution channel and visual art layer

It is a permanent, year-round creative space (physical + digital) in Dumaguete where filmmakers work with AI tools to produce short films, experimental cinema, and documentary content.

## 2. What It Produces

- Short narrative films (3–15 min) with AI-generated visuals and practical filming
- Fully AI-generated animated/visual shorts (no camera needed)
- Documentary films with AI narration, subtitles, and color grading
- AI-scored music (from Music Pipeline) + synchronized visual content
- "Neuromicon Cinema" — films set in the Neuromicon narrative universe

## 3. Tools and Pipeline

```
Script / storyboard (human)
  → Image generation: Recraft.ai / Ideogram for stills and style references
  → Video generation: RunwayML Gen-3 / Kling AI for motion sequences
  → Voice/narration: ElevenLabs
  → Music: Suno + Udio (AI Music Pipeline)
  → Edit: VEED.io / DaVinci Resolve
  → Subtitle: Whisper (OpenAI) auto-transcription
  → Publish: IPFS archive + UUCP screening + FilmFreeway submission
```

## 4. Community Model

AI Cinema Lab is not a solo production studio. It operates as:
- **Open workshop** — any Dumaguete filmmaker can use tools and techniques
- **Collaborative production** — credits shared, Fair BEAT applies to any revenue
- **UUCP integration** — Lab productions get a dedicated UUCP showcase slot
- **FilmFreeway submissions** — international festival circuit for best Lab works

## 5. Elena Vasquez Integration

Elena Vasquez (UUCP curator, trust level 4) has expressed strong interest in developing an AI film category for UUCP Season 3. AI Cinema Lab formalizes this into a permanent institutional identity within UUCP.
