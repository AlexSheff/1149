# AI Music Pipeline — White Paper

> *A systematic AI-generated music production and distribution engine — from prompt to Spotify, autonomously.*

## 1. The Problem

Independent music production is bottlenecked by:
- Studio time costs (removed by AI)
- Technical production skills (abstracted by AI)
- Distribution complexity (solved by DistroKid/RouteNote)
- Marketing budget (partially addressable via community + AI content)

What remains is: creative direction, quality curation, and systematic release cadence.

## 2. The Vision

Build a continuously active AI music library across all major streaming platforms. The pipeline is:
- **Generative** — AI creates the music from creative prompts and direction
- **Curatorial** — human creative direction decides what gets released
- **Systematic** — consistent release cadence, not sporadic drops
- **Monetized** — multiple revenue streams, tracked and reinvested
- **Expandable** — grows toward an AI Music Collective with Fair BEAT revenue distribution

## 4. Distribution Stack

| Destination | Service | Revenue Type |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon | DistroKid | Streaming royalties |
| YouTube Music, Deezer, Tidal | RouteNote | Royalty-free / streaming |
| SoundCloud | Direct | Community / preview |
| Sync licensing | Pond5 / Artlist | One-time or recurring fees |
| Direct sales | Bandcamp | Fan direct |
| Supporter subscriptions | Patreon / Ko-fi | Monthly recurring |

## 5. Release Strategy

### Weekly Cadence (target)
- 2–3 tracks per week minimum
- 1 music video per 2 weeks
- 1 "concept EP" per month (5–7 tracks with unified aesthetic)

### Genre Portfolio Approach
Maintain releases across 4–5 genre pools simultaneously:
- Ambient / atmospheric (long streams, playlist friendly)
- Electronic / synthwave (high engagement)
- World / fusion (Dumaguete / Philippines cultural identity)
- Experimental / Neuromicon-lore music (brand identity)

## 6. Fair BEAT Integration (Future Phase)

Phase 2: Establish AI Music Collective.
- 3–5 AI music producers pool releases under collective identity
- Revenue flows through Fair BEAT smart contract
- Distribution: proportional to trust score in `music/collective` context
- Governance: collective members vote on release schedule, branding, reinvestment
