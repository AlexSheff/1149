# City Quest — White Paper

> *A multi-app global citizenship platform where solidarity is structurally embedded in every transaction.*

---

## 1. The Problem

Modern civic life is fragmented. People participate in work, community, art, governance, and ecology through systems that are siloed, extractive, and designed to capture attention rather than build commons. Cities have no operating system. Citizens have no coordination layer. Solidarity remains an intention, not a mechanism.

## 2. The Vision

City Quest is a 14-app ecosystem that gives citizens a full suite of tools to participate in, contribute to, and benefit from local and global civic life — with Fair BEAT redistribution embedded in every economic interaction.

Think of it as an operating system for a city built on solidarity.

## 3. The 14 Apps

### Core Apps
| App | Function |
|-----|----------|
| Choose&Own | Civic ownership and democratic participation mechanics |
| JobAround | Hyperlocal work marketplace |
| Guardians | Civic protection and ethical action network |
| Press Club | Independent community journalism |
| Art Maze | Creative economy and artistic collaboration |

### Service Layer
| App | Function |
|-----|----------|
| ImInGame | Participation tracking and civic gamification |
| MeetMap | Geospatial community event coordination |
| Duma inWork | Local governance participation |
| Recycle Economy | Circular economy and recycling incentives |
| Fair BEAT | Redistribution engine (embedded in all above) |

### Additional
ImInGame extension, social layer, onboarding, admin tools, API layer
