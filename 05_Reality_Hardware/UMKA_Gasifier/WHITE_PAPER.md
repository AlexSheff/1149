# UMKA Gasifier — White Paper

> *Small-scale biomass gasification for off-grid community energy — waste to power, locally controlled.*

---

## 1. Concept

Small-scale biomass gasifier converting agricultural and forestry waste into syngas for community power generation.

**Status: PAUSED** — Awaiting physical space and initial capital for prototype build. Design is complete; execution requires conditions not yet available.

---

## 2. Why Dumaguete?

- Abundant biomass feedstock: coconut husks, rice straw, wood chips (all abundant in Local Region)
- Frequent power interruptions creating real demand for alternatives
- City Quest Recycle Economy creates a biomass collection stream
- Off-grid energy aligns with digital sovereignty principles from layer 04

---

## 3. Energy Independence as Solidarity

Communities dependent on grid power are structurally extractable. Energy independence removes a key vector of control. This is not primarily a technical project — it is a **sovereignty project** with technical implementation.

---

## 4. Target Specifications

| Parameter | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Electrical output | 5–20 kW per unit |
| Feedstock consumption | 15–30 kg/hr dry biomass |
| Feedstock types | Coconut husks, rice straw, wood chips |
| Deployment scale | Community / neighborhood (50–200 households) |
