# Manifesto of the Free World Citizen

> *A declaration of global citizenship — allegiance to humanity, home on the planet, community built from chosen bonds.*

---

## Preamble

I am not a Origin Nationality citizen, a Host Nationality resident, or a digital nomad. I am a **Free World Citizen** — a person whose primary allegiance is to humanity, whose home is the planet, and whose community is built from chosen bonds, not inherited ones.

This manifesto is the social interface of CODE 1149. It defines how I engage with every person, community, and system I encounter.

---

## Declaration

**1. Borders are administrative, not moral.**  
I respect laws within jurisdictions while rejecting the premise that birthplace determines worth. A person born in the Philippines is not more or less valuable than a person born in Russia, the United States, or anywhere else.

**2. Language is technology.**  
I use language to build bridges, not walls. Origin Nationality, English, Local Language — each language is an access protocol to a different model of reality. Fluency in multiple languages is cognitive diversity, not divided loyalty.

**3. Community is chosen.**  
I live in Dumaguete not by accident but by deliberate decision. The act of *choosing* a place is the deepest form of belonging. Roots chosen are stronger than roots inherited.

**4. Solidarity travels.**  
The redistribution mechanisms I build here must work anywhere. Local implementation, global architecture. If it only works in Dumaguete, the design is incomplete.

**5. The question is the compass.**  
*"Who am I in the context of the universe?"* — this question does not have a national answer. It has a human one.

---

## Obligations of Free World Citizenship

Citizenship without borders does not mean citizenship without responsibility. It means the opposite: **expanded responsibility**.

- To contribute to every community I inhabit
- To build systems that outlast my presence
- To share knowledge without gatekeeping
- To refuse extraction in every form I can control

---

## Application to This Repository

Every project in this repository is built by a Free World Citizen for a world of Free World Citizens. The local anchor (Dumaguete) is the proving ground. The global architecture is the goal.
