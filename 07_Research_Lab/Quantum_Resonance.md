# Quantum Resonance Models

> *Speculative models connecting quantum mechanics, gravity, and consciousness as aspects of a unified information system.*

---

## 1. The Central Hypothesis

The universe is an information-processing system. Gravity, consciousness, and quantum phenomena are not separate domains — they are different **resolution levels** of the same underlying computational substrate.

This is not a fringe position. It connects several serious research threads:

---

## 2. Research Threads

### Information-Theoretic Gravity

Jacobson (1995) derived Einstein's field equations from thermodynamics — suggesting gravity *emerges* from entropic principles rather than being fundamental. Verlinde (2010) extended this with entropic gravity.

The implication: if gravity emerges from information processing, the medium of that processing is the universe itself.

### Integrated Information Theory (IIT)

Tononi's IIT proposes that consciousness is identical to integrated information (Φ). Every system with non-zero Φ has some degree of experience. A very simple system has very simple experience. The universe, as the maximally integrated system, would have maximal experience.

If correct, we are not observers in a mechanical universe. We are local intensifications of a consciousness that was always there.

### Quantum Cognition

Quantum formalism applied to cognitive phenomena — not necessarily requiring physical quantum effects in neurons, but using quantum mathematics to describe belief superposition, conceptual entanglement, and decision interference.

## 3. The 1149 Connection

The question *"Who am I in the context of the universe?"* becomes, in this framework:

*What is the relationship between this local information-processing system (a person) and the global information-processing system (the universe)?*

If IIT is correct, the answer is: **the same process, at different scales.**
