# UUCP Film Festival — White Paper

> *Interactive cinema as a collective intelligence trainer — film as a tool for community consciousness expansion.*

---

## 1. Beyond Entertainment

The UUCP Film Festival is not a film screening event. It is a **collective intelligence training program** that uses cinema as the medium.

Film creates shared experience. Shared experience creates shared vocabulary. Shared vocabulary enables collective decision-making that is otherwise impossible across diverse groups.

---

## 2. Season Status

**Season 3 underway.**  
Season 2 (Feb–Mar 2026, Dumaguete): **47 films screened, ~300 attendees.**  
FilmFreeway submissions open for Season 3 main program.

---

## 3. Programming Model

- **Primary curator:** Elena Vasquez
- **Submission route:** FilmFreeway international circuit
- **Selection:** trust-weighted community panel (5 curators minimum)
- **Target split:** 60% international / 40% local + regional
- **Venue:** Dumaguete Cultural Center + partner spaces
- **Online:** selected films available 30 days post-screening

---

## 4. AI Cinema Category (Season 3 New)

A dedicated programming track for films with ≥30% AI-generated visual content:
- Attribution mandatory (which tools used, what human creative direction)
- Judged independently from traditional shorts
- AI Cinema Lab is the Dumaguete incubator for this category

---

## 5. FilmFreeway Circuit

- ~200 international submissions per season target
- Official selection laurels and jury awards
- Building Dumaguete as a named destination in independent cinema

---

## 6. Economics

Entry fees + ticket revenue → 50% venue/ops / 30% filmmaker honoraria / 20% City Quest treasury.  
**Filmmaker honoraria are guaranteed regardless of revenue.** Film culture should not depend on charity.

---

## 7. The Interactive Dimension

Post-screening discussions are structured as collective reasoning exercises. Films are chosen not just for quality but for **cognitive challenge** — they should leave the audience productively confused and curious.
